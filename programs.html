Iron Forge

Iron Forge is an intensive strength program designed to build muscle mass and increase strength. This program focuses on classic strength exercises such as squats, bench presses, deadlifts, and military presses. It consists of high-volume training with moderate to high intensity, emphasizing progressive overload and achieving new personal records. Iron Forge will challenge you and stimulate muscle growth and strength improvement.

Titan's Wrath

Titan's Wrath is an advanced strength program that combines traditional and functional exercises to challenge your body and promote muscle growth. This program is based on principles of supersets, drop sets, and pyramid training, providing variety and intensity. It includes complex exercises such as kettlebell deadlifts, Bulgarian split squats, weighted pull-ups, and farmer's walks. Titan's Wrath will push you out of your comfort zone and help you achieve a new level of strength and endurance.

Inferno Blitz

Inferno Blitz is an intense CrossFit workout that combines various functional exercises and cardiovascular elements to challenge the body and improve endurance. This training involves a series of rapid circuits consisting of exercises such as burpees, box jumps, kettlebell swings, and skippings. With short rests between circuits, Inferno Blitz will test your strength, endurance, and mental determination.

Titan's Gauntlet

Titan's Gauntlet is a complex CrossFit workout that combines elements of strength, endurance, and agility to challenge the entire body. This training includes a series of exercises such as deadlifts, thrusters, pull-ups, box jumps, and double unders, performed in succession for a set time or number of repetitions. Titan's Gauntlet provides a dynamic and intense workout that will push you out of your comfort zone and inspire you to achieve new fitness goals.
